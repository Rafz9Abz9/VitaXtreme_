Shipping Information

How will my parcel be delivered?

Your parcel will be delivered using reliable and efficient shipping services. We partner with trusted carriers to ensure your order reaches you in a timely manner. You'll receive a tracking number once your order is shipped, allowing you to monitor its progress and estimated delivery date. Rest assured, we prioritize the safe and secure delivery of your self-care essentials.

Do I pay for delivery?

Yes, delivery fees may apply and will be calculated during the checkout process based on your location and the shipping method selected. Keep an eye out for any promotions or free shipping offers that may be available. We strive to offer transparent and fair shipping costs to ensure a seamless shopping experience for our customers.

My item has become faulty

We apologize for any inconvenience. Please contact our customer support team with your order number and details about the issue. We'll work swiftly to assess the situation and provide you with the necessary assistance, whether it involves a replacement, refund, or another appropriate solution. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we're here to ensure your self-care experience is nothing short of exceptional.

Orders and Returns

Tracking my order

After your order is processed and shipped, you will receive an email containing a tracking number. Use this tracking number on our website's "Track Order" page or follow the link provided in the email to monitor your package's delivery status. For further assistance or inquiries, don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team. We're here to ensure your order arrives smoothly and on time.

How can I return an item?

If, for any reason, you are dissatisfied with your purchase, you can return any items that are unopened and unused within 30 days of receiving them. Please ensure that all packaging, cellophane wrapping, and tamper-resistant seals remain intact; otherwise, the item will be deemed unsuitable for resale and will not be accepted by our returns department.

Payments

What payment methods can I use?

We accept credit and debit cards.

Has my payment gone through?

Upon successful completion of your order, you should receive a confirmation email, which serves as acknowledgment that your payment has been successfully processed. If you haven't received this confirmation, please check your email spam folder. Additionally, you can log in to your account on our website to view your order history and payment status. If you have any concerns or need further assistance, feel free to contact our customer support team and we'll be happy to help.
